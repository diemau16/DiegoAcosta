Could media be controlling election results?

Nowadays everyone can spread information through social media, whether it's true or not; one thing I'm going to talk about is fake news. Fake news has been used for thousands of years by many cultures and for different purposes. This is a problem we haven't get rid of, in fact, it is now becoming a bigger trouble; known journals are sharing more and more fake news, making people lose their confidence in the news. Therefore, every newspaper that spreads fake news or publish biased information should be severely punished.

First, media is influencing political and social events. For example, some journals give a favorable or unfavorable image to one specific person or ideologies, since they can show whatever they want to people with no regulations. They also often associate with the government itself to work as their allies in elections and all kinds of political and social events. They basically can control who's in power and which decisions are going to be taken.

On the other hand, some may say that regulating the information shared is against freedom of expression. However, we can reduce the damage fake news produce controlling the information objectively so that no popular journal can persuade people. There is a lot of people who take seriously whatever they see on the internet or the news and will perform accordingly. That's another reason to control what journals can share, so that people will participate in political events more objectively since they will be well informed from trusted sources. This proves that fake news in general can affect every single person one way or another.

In conclusion, there should be a law that prohibits the use of fake news. Mostly because media is more involved in political issues and people's everyday life than it should. Hopefully, we can accomplish this mission so that it will be easier to progress as a society once we get rid of fake news.
